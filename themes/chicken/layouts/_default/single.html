BEHIND THE BLOG

TRANG TRAN

Montréal,Canada | Influencer| Book Blogger | Food Blogger

Hi y'all! Pastel Cravings is the combination of my love for anything pastel, the light color that you can find in almost every dessert and my coup de foudre aka love of my life : food. I've been in the blogging platform for almost 3 years now and it's so much fun that I told myself, why not do one for food? That's how Pastel Cravings was born!

Thank god, I'm not the only foodie in town! I've asked some of my favourite girls to contribute and give you guys more variety in the food industry (because honestly if I'm the only one who blogs about food it won't be healthy LOL)
