Hey there!

My name is Laura - but some people call me Lauray. I’m a Dietetics/Nutrition student, a travel junkie, an art-lover and an aspiring food blogger (check out my health and lifestyle blog @colourmypalate)! Although I grew up and live in Montreal’s version of suburbia #westielyfe, like most people, I have fallen in love with the city and everything it has to offer. I’m excited to share with you all my discoveries!
